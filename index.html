Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Steps:

  • Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  • Heat a pan and pour the batter.
  • Cook until golden on both sides.
  • Serve with syrup or fruits.
